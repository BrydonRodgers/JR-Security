FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide security services in Loeriesfontein?
Yes — Loeriesfontein is our home base. We provide security guarding, armed response, CCTV installation, and cleaning services throughout Loeriesfontein and the Hantam district, including Carnarvon, Calvinia, Brandvlei, and Kenhardt.
What other areas do you serve in South Africa?
We serve the broader Northern Cape (Kimberley, Upington, Springbok, De Aar, Kuruman) and Western Cape (Cape Town, Stellenbosch, George, Paarl, Mossel Bay). Contact us to confirm coverage in your specific area.
Are your security guards PSIRA registered?
Yes. All our security guards are fully PSIRA (Private Security Industry Regulatory Authority) registered and compliant with South African security industry standards. We maintain strict compliance with all regulations.
Do you offer 24/7 armed response?
Yes. Our emergency control room operates around the clock. Armed response teams are strategically positioned for rapid deployment. Emergency line: 087 822 2981 — available 24/7.
How quickly can you start service?
For security guarding and cleaning, we can typically begin within 24–48 hours. CCTV and alarm installations are quoted within 48 hours of your request. Contact us via WhatsApp for the fastest response.
