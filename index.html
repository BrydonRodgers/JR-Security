FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve in South Africa?
We provide security and cleaning services throughout the Northern Cape (Kimberley, Upington, Springbok, De Aar, Kuruman) and Western Cape (Cape Town, Stellenbosch, George, Paarl, Mossel Bay) provinces of South Africa.
Are your security guards PSIRA registered?
Yes, all our security guards are fully PSIRA (Private Security Industry Regulatory Authority) registered and compliant with South African security industry standards. We maintain strict compliance with all PSIRA regulations.
Do you offer 24/7 armed response?
Yes, we provide 24/7 armed response services across Northern Cape and Western Cape. Our emergency control room operates around the clock, and our armed response teams are strategically positioned for rapid deployment.
How quickly can you start service?
For standard security guarding and cleaning services, we can typically begin within 24-48 hours. For specialized installations like CCTV systems, we provide a free assessment and quote within 48 hours of your request.
